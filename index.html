Introduction

Our Earth is drowning in pollution — yet profit too often takes priority over the planet. Every day, tons of plastic and non-biodegradable waste end up in landfills, oceans, and even inside animals. That’s why we created RetroUse, powered by Google’s Gemini AI. With just your API key, RetroUse scans and detects recyclable items in real time.

The Problem

It’s not just about plastic. It’s about all harmful, non-biodegradable polymers that threaten ecosystems. This isn’t someone else’s problem — it’s our problem. Earth’s problem. The continued neglect of recycling is silently destroying the environment.

The Solution

We don’t need more posters or campaigns that people ignore. We need action. RetroUse transforms passive observers into active changemakers by showing how waste can be recycled, reused, or even upcycled into something new and useful.

Recycle

Scan an item, identify it, and get recycling & upcycling instructions instantly.

Reuse

Get creative and efficient ways to reuse your waste instead of throwing it away.

ReEarth

Learn methods to grow plants, trees, and contribute to a greener planet.

How to Use

  1. Download RetroUse from our website.

  2. Get your Google API key from Google Cloud Console.

  3. Open the app and enter your API key in the popup.

  4. Choose from 4 modes: Recycle, Reuse, Reduce, or ReEarth.

Future Vision

RetroUse is not just another awareness app. It’s the beginning of a larger revolution — a future where waste fuels innovation, and every discarded item is a chance to give back to Earth. The future of this planet depends on solutions like RetroUse — and on people who choose to use them.
